# Weekly Meal Templates

## Default 7-Day Dinner Plan

| Day       | Meal                | Key Ingredients                            | Time   |
|-----------|---------------------|--------------------------------------------|--------|
| Monday    | Pasta Bolognese     | pasta, ground beef, tomatoes, garlic       | 30 min |
| Tuesday   | Chicken Stir Fry    | chicken, bell pepper, broccoli, rice       | 25 min |
| Wednesday | Lentil Soup         | lentils, carrots, celery, onion            | 20 min |
| Thursday  | Salmon & Potatoes   | salmon, potatoes, lemon, dill              | 35 min |
| Friday    | Homemade Pizza      | dough, tomato sauce, mozzarella            | 40 min |
| Saturday  | BBQ Chicken         | chicken, bbq sauce, corn                   | 30 min |
| Sunday    | Roast Vegetables    | zucchini, bell pepper, feta, olive oil     | 25 min |

## Adaptation Rules

- If vegetarian preference set: replace meat meals (Bolognese → pasta primavera, Chicken → tofu stir fry)
- If budget < €70: skip salmon (expensive), add more lentil/egg meals
- Always prioritize using fridge contents first
